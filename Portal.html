Portal …

is a video game series released in 2007 by Valve software. The games are first-person puzzle games. The games revolve around the player, who weilds a special gun that can create to portals that are interconnected.

In-game screenshot

Using the gun to their advantage, players must solve test chambers at Aperture science under the watchful eye of GLaDOS, an AI build to maintain the facility. Accoridng to Portal's Wikipedia Page, the game uses ideas from Narbacular Drop , which was the prototype for this game. Most of the staff that worked on Narbacular Drop ended up getting hired at Valve in order to further their prototype, which became Portal.
